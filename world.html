üìñ Travel Journal: Japan

Japan exceeded all my expectations. From the moment I stepped into the organized chaos of Shibuya Crossing to the serene temples of Kyoto, every moment was magical. Paris is everything they say it is - romantic, artistic, and utterly captivating. Walking along the Seine at sunset, exploring hidden bookshops in the Latin Quarter, and tasting fresh pastries at every corner made me fall in love with this city.
